Demountable Partitions

ESPAI demountable office partition on the basis of aluminum sections can be used in design projects of any complexity. The decorative elements give the partition a highly aesthetic look. And due to numerous options of profiles filling, design opportunities of this system are truly unlimited. They provide for high-quality joints of minimal area and ensure common style of interior solutions. Espai office partition customised shopfronts and glazed screens in a variety of finishes to suit customer tastes and design specifications. Suited to virtually every office environment, ESPAI Partitions may be single or double glazed as required and can be supplied with a choice of office doors ranging from the conventional hinged timber type doors to sliding glass doors and flush double glazed partitioning. Maximize space for minimal cost with office partitions made of the highest quality tempered glass. Improve the productivity and atmosphere with a variety of designs, glass types and frame finishes.





ESPAI Glass Partition

Flexibility is a hallmark of our office partitions. Walls can open, close or be made stationary with the touch of a lever. Choices of finishes, sizes, glass types, frame styles and hardware are practically endless.
Frame Finishes: Our frames are made of the highest quality aluminum, are virtually maintenance free and are 100% recyclable. Various frame colors allow you to match or contrast with other interiors. 6-12mm tempered glass is standard on all Space Plus doors. 9mm laminated glass is also available. Glass is available in clear, frosted, laminated and combinations. 100% recyclable.
Handles And Locks: Lever handle and lock system for swing doors. Handles in various sizes. Flush indent handles with key locks available. Slider hook latch/lock with key (has master key capabilities). Flush slide bolt allows a panel to be stationary when needed for privacy and unlocked at any time from the inside.
