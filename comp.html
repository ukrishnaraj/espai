BRING THE OUTSIDE IN

ESPAI MIDDLE EAST (FZE) established in 2013 as the Middle East subsidiary of Espai Sistemas, Spain & Airclos Spain, has evolved into a leading manufacturer & designer of interior solutions with our own high quality systems and original designs to adapt with the changing market needs. The aim of Espai Middle East is to continue the company's sustained growth within the same parameters that have guided our success until now.


Our focus is on the development and manufacture of special aluminum systems such as folding sliding doors, frameless balcony glazing systems, automatic retractable roofing systems, Demountable partition, and Frameless shower doors that meets the European standard. We are a leading company in the design and manufacture of folding, sliding, roofing systems,Demountable partition and Frameless shower doors which have been fully developed and are capable of providing the best solutions to meet any requirement. We offer our customers the most innovative, best quality, advanced technology and the best eco-friendly products, on the strength of differentiated European designs, which is very compatible with the Middle East weather conditions.
